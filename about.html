Why I built SplitPDF

I was frustrated with online PDF tools that require you to upload sensitive documents to unknown servers. SplitPDF was built to solve that: split your PDF pages in complete privacy, entirely inside your own browser, with no files ever leaving your device.

How it works

SplitPDF uses PDF.js to render page previews and pdf-lib to reassemble pages into new PDF files — all via WebAssembly and JavaScript running locally in your tab. Nothing is sent to a server.

  • Drop or select a PDF file
  • Pick individual pages, every page, or custom ranges
  • Drag to reorder pages before exporting
  • Download your split PDF(s) instantly

The full suite

SplitPDF is part of a small family of free, privacy-first PDF tools:

  • FreeMergePDF — merge multiple PDFs into one
  • FreeCompressPDF — reduce PDF file size
  • ConvertToPDFFree — convert images to PDF
